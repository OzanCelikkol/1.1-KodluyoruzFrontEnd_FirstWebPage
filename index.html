    • MARTIN EDEN / JACK LONDON

      Martin Eden is a 1909 novel by American author Jack London about a young proletarian autodidact

      struggling to become a writer. It was first serialized in The Pacific Monthly magazine from September

      The broad narrative of Hitchhiker follows the misadventures of the last surviving man,

      way for a hyperspace bypass.

    • THE LORD OF THE RINGS / J. R. R. TOLKIEN

      The Lord of the Rings is the saga of a group of sometimes reluctant heroes who set

      forth to save their world from consummate evil. Its many worlds and creatures were drawn

      from Tolkien's extensive knowledge of philology and folklore.
